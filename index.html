What I Do

Every visit, three parts.

01

Consultation

Every visit starts with a consultation. I listen to what you wish, preferably look at your favourite haircut image. Then we talk through the possibilities together. The result is intentional, not guesswork. You'll leave feeling like yourself, only sharper.

02

Cutting

Whether it's a basic haircut or a fully individual style, I work efficiently and safely, typically within thirty minutes. I use modern tools of the highest standard, and my practice meets Canadian Hairstyling and Barbering sanitary regulations, verified through regular official inspections.

03

The Shop Experience

Clean, professional, and quietly welcoming. Relaxing music plays as you settle in, giving you a genuine chance to step away from the noise of your day. However briefly, this is a place to be looked after, not processed.

About

About Lucyna.

I started cutting hair at five years old — much to the amusement and occasional alarm of my family. Dolls came first, then increasingly brave relatives and friends who, somehow, kept coming back. Years later, that early instinct became a formal trade: I trained and certified through the Ontario College of Trades and the Ministry of Training, Colleges and Universities.

My path also runs through architecture and interior design, and I bring that same sensibility to the stylist chair — an eye for proportion, detail, and how a space or a person might feel. I've learned that my clients are not just looking for a good haircut, they're looking for thirty honest, unhurried minutes in a demanding week.
